Search Console MCP includes full support for **Bing Webmaster Tools**. You can analyze Bing traffic, find ranking opportunities, monitor crawl issues, and submit URLs — all through the same AI agent you use for Google.

## What You Can Do

### Search Performance
Query your Bing search data the same way you would with Google — clicks, impressions, CTR, and position by keyword, page, country, or device.

### SEO Opportunities
Find low-hanging fruit keywords on Bing, get prioritized SEO recommendations, identify **lost queries**, and analyze **brand vs non-brand** performance — all tuned for Bing's ranking behavior.

### Crawl Monitoring
See what Bing's crawler is finding on your site — broken pages, redirect chains, and other crawl issues that could hurt your rankings.

### URL Submission & IndexNow
Submit URLs directly to Bing's index or use **IndexNow** for instant notifications when your content changes. No waiting for Bing to discover your updates.

### Site Health
Run health checks on your Bing properties to catch issues before they affect your traffic.
